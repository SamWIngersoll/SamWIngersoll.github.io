MKC Motorsports

Racing Team and Workshop, South Carolina

MKC Motorsports is a startup Race team founded in 2022 by RC driver and enthusiast Sam Ingersoll (AKA "Quickdraw")

MKC Motorsports has several teams currently racing in carpet oval, carpet road, asphalt super oval, and off road oval. The #31 carpet oval streetsock has been the most succesful team, securing multiple podium finishes. During the Fall and Spring, MKC Motorsports races at website in both the Streetstock and Pan car breakout class.

MKC Motorsports is the next up and coming RC race team.
